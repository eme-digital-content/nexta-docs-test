# Welcome to PubliFast

**PubliFast** is a Content Management System (CMS) designed to simplify the creation and publication of blog posts. It offers an intuitive interface and robust features, allowing you to focus on crafting high-quality content that resonates with your audience, without worrying about technicalities.

![PubliFast Logo with Tagline](/images/publifast-logo-sub.png)

---

## Key features

With the **PubliFast** interface, you can:

- [Create and publish](/publifast-documentation/publishing-content/create-new-post) posts.
- [Edit your posts](/publifast-documentation/publishing-content/edit-post) as needed.
- [Schedule posts](/publifast-documentation/publishing-content/schedule-post) to publish later.
- Delete [a post](/publifast-documentation/publishing-content/delete-post) or [all your content](/publifast-documentation/publishing-content/delete-all-content).
- Manage [pages](/publifast-documentation/publishing-content/manage-pages) and [tags](/publifast-documentation/publishing-content/manage-tags) for your blog.
- Add members to your audience, [individually](/publifast-documentation/manage-account/add-new-member) or [in a bulk](/publifast-documentation/manage-account/add-members-bulk).
- Manage all your [account settings](/publifast-documentation/manage-account/edit-settings).

Additionally, you are able to configure and use more advanced options such as [Memberships](https://tech-writer-test.netlify.app/advanced-configurations/memberships), [Analytics](https://tech-writer-test.netlify.app/advanced-configurations/analytics), and [Newsletter](https://tech-writer-test.netlify.app/advanced-configurations/newsletter).
