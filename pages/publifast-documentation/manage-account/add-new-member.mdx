## How to add a new member

The **Members** feature allows you to build an audience for your blog, while enabling you to turn any site into a membership business with member signup, paid subscriptions, and email newsletters.

This guide covers how to add a new member manually. Follow the instructions below.

1. Access your project in [PubliFast](https://account.ghost.org/signin).
2. In the left menu, click the **Members** option.
   - This will display a list of all the current members of your audience.

![Select members option](public/images/prints-ui/members/add-member-home.png)

3. Click the **New member** button, located in the upper-right corner.

![Add new member button](public/images/prints-ui/members/add-new-member.png)

4. In the form, you can add all the information related to the new member.

   - **Name**: The name of the member.
   - **Email**: A email address to contact and send content to the memeber.
   - **Labels**: Labels to identify the group the members belong to, such as paid subscriptions and subscription tiers.
   - **Note**: A commentary about the member. This wil not visible to the member. You can write up to _500 characters_.
   - **Newletters**: Switches to enable/disable the member subscriptions to your newsletters.
   - **Activity**: once the new member is created, you will receive events and metrics related to this member activity.

5. Click the **Save** button, located in the upper-right corner.

Done! The new member has been added to your database.
